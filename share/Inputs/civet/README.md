# CIVET models

The midthickness and white surfaces were obtained directly from CIVET.

Spherical surfaces were obtained via:

```bash
mris_smooth -n 3 -nw ${white} ${smoothwm}
mris_inflate ${smoothwm} ${inflated}
mris_sphere ${inflated} ${sphere}
```

(where `${smoothwm}` and `${inflated}` are temporary files discarded after use.)

Inflated and very inflated surfaces were obtained via:

```bash
wb_command -surface-generate-inflated ${midthickness} ${inflated} ${veryinflated}
```

Sulcal depth maps are the average of the sulcal depth maps from all HCP subjects processed through CIVET and projected to the relevant surface densities.

Average vertex area maps (`desc-vaavg`) were calculated from the midthickness surface of all HCP subjects processed through CIVET.

The `tpl-civet_space-fsLR` maps were generated by using a two-step MSMsulc registration on all HCP subjects and then averaging the registered spherical meshes.
The `tpl-civet_space-fsaverage` maps were generated by rotating the `space-fsLR` maps using the fsLR -> fsaverage mapping provided by FreeSurfer.
